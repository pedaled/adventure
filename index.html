// About

Rider. Analyst.
Wanderer.

Based in Skellefteå, Sweden — I spend my days working with Nordic electricity markets and my evenings planning the next ride.

This site is a journal of where the roads take me. Long-distance bikepacking, gravel routes through Scandinavia, and in May 2026 — a 4,500km loop through Italy covering Sardinia, Sicily and the mainland.

Expect route notes, gear lists, maps pulled from Strava, and the occasional data deep-dive.
